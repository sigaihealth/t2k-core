# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants must communicate professionally, critique ideas rather than
people, respect different experience levels, and avoid harassment,
discrimination, threats, or disclosure of private information.

## Enforcement

For a confidential report, use the repository's private security-reporting
channel and identify the submission as a conduct concern. Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior threatens a safe and productive project.

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a
reporter or participant in an investigation is prohibited.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when a person publicly represents the
project. Good-faith disagreement over technical or governance decisions is not
misconduct; personal attacks and harassment are.
